# Research Areas

## 1. CSI-based Human Activity Recognition (HAR)
Using WiFi Channel State Information for fine-grained activity recognition, fall detection, and multimodal fusion with vision systems.

## 2. AI/ML for Wireless Communications
Applying LLMs, VLMs, LSTMs, and advanced AI architectures for wireless sensing, link prediction, and network intelligence.

## 3. mmWave & 6G System Modeling
Simulation and modeling of mmWave beamforming, multi-link prediction, and sub-THz channel characterization.

## 4. Symbolic Regression & Temporal Modeling
Using LTC networks, LSTM models, and symbolic regression to explain and interpret wireless system behavior.
