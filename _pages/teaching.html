Teaching Experience

Below is a summary of my teaching and instructional experience at San Jose State University in the Department of Electrical Engineering. I have served as an Instructional Student Assistant (ISA) for multiple graduate-level EE courses in Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in a hands-on, application-driven approach to teaching. My goal is to help students build strong intuition in:

  • Machine Learning & Neural Networks
  • Computer Vision & AI Applications
  • Python & MATLAB programming for engineers
  • Deep Learning model design, debugging, and evaluation

I emphasize conceptual clarity, real-world applicability, and strong technical communication in all my teaching roles.
